Common questions

What is the monthly maintenance for?
The monthly fee covers hosting management, security updates, backups, content changes, and technical support. Think of it like a building maintenance contract — your website stays safe, fast, and up-to-date without you lifting a finger.
Do I need to buy hosting separately?
Yes — hosting and domain are paid directly by you (roughly ₹3,000–5,000/year). We guide you through everything and set it all up for you. This way the hosting is fully in your name and ownership.
Can I upgrade from Basic to Standard later?
Absolutely. You can upgrade anytime by paying the difference in the setup fee. All your existing content moves over — nothing is lost.
What if I want to stop the monthly plan?
No lock-in. Cancel anytime with 30 days' notice. Your website and all its files remain yours — we'll hand over everything cleanly.
How long does it take to build my website?
Basic: 5–7 days. Standard: 7–10 days. Pro: 12–18 days. Timeline starts after you provide your content (photos, text, logo). We'll guide you on exactly what we need.
